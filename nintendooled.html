Product Details

The Nintendo Switch OLED model offers a striking 7-inch OLED screen with vibrant colors and deep contrast, significantly enhancing the handheld gaming experience. The system supports both handheld and TV modes, with a maximum resolution of 1080p when docked. A new dock includes a LAN port for more stable online play, and the battery life ranges from 4.5 to 9 hours depending on usage.
