Playstation 5 Slim (Disc Edition)

RM 2,499

Product Details

The PS5 Slim Disc Edition is a more compact and lighter version of the original PS5, featuring an Ultra HD Blu-ray drive for physical game discs and media playback. It offers the same core performance as the original model, powered by a custom AMD Ryzen "Zen 2" CPU and RDNA 2 GPU, delivering up to 10.3 TFLOPS of graphical power with ray tracing and support for 4K gaming at up to 120fps. It includes a 1TB ultra-high-speed SSD for faster load times, with additional storage expansion via an M.2 slot. This model retains immersive features like 3D audio and haptic feedback through the DualSense controller. The PS5 Slim Disc Edition has a modular design, allowing users to remove panels for customization or replacement.
