Nintendo Switch (Lite)

RM 838

Product Details

The Nintendo Switch Lite is a compact, handheld-only gaming system that features a 5.5-inch LCD screen with 720p resolution, offering a lightweight and portable way to enjoy Nintendo Switch games, though it cannot be docked to a TV or used with detachable Joy-Con controllers, making it ideal for those who prefer on-the-go gaming. Available in multiple colors including blue, yellow, gray, turquoise, and coral, it offers a battery life of 3 to 7 hours, depending on the game, and supports multiplayer through local wireless connections or online play with a Nintendo Switch Online membership. While it retains the same processing power as other Switch models, it’s best suited for players focused on handheld gaming and those looking for a more affordable option without TV compatibility.
