Xbox (Series S)

Product Details

The Xbox Series S is a compact, all-digital gaming console offering an affordable entry point into next-generation gaming. It features a custom AMD Zen 2 CPU, RDNA 2 GPU with 4 TFLOPS of graphical power, and a high-speed NVMe SSD for significantly reduced load times and enhanced performance. Targeting 1080p to 1440p resolution, it supports up to 120 FPS in select games and includes features like ray tracing and variable refresh rates. The Series S is now available in configurations with either a 512GB or 1TB SSD, although limited storage may require external expansion for larger game libraries.
